Membership

Co-op membership is open to any person or organization subject to the approval of our directors. To become a member, fill out the following form and send payment of $20 CAD equivalent in Bitcoin to the address provided.

Once approved, you will be given a member number and recorded in our membership registry, giving you the ability to nominate and elect directors at our annual general meetings and vote on resolutions. Please read our bylaws and memorandum of agreement to learn more about how we operate.
